# K12 Signature Building

**K12 Signature Building** is a configurable email signature generator for K-12 school districts.

Administrators can configure district branding, colors, logos, buildings, phone numbers, legal notices, accessibility options, and signature layouts. The builder can then export a standalone Staff Generator that employees use to create consistent district-approved email signatures.

## What's New in v6.1.4

- Moved **Extension** into **Building & Contact**.
- Renamed **Desk Phone** to **Phone Number**.
- Moved **Include QR Code** into **Website & Scheduling**.
- Kept the v6.1.2 WCAG/ADA color contrast tools and hosted-logo warnings.

## Included Files

- `K12_Signature_Building_Builder.html` - administrator builder and staff generator exporter
- `docs/K12_Signature_Building_User_Guide.pdf` - setup and use directions
- `config/sample-district-config.json` - sample configuration
- `CHANGELOG.md` - version history
- `LICENSE.txt` - license

## Typical Workflow

1. Open `K12_Signature_Building_Builder.html` in a modern browser.
2. Configure district information, colors, logos, buildings, and notices.
3. Upload logos when possible so they are embedded and portable.
4. Export the Staff Generator.
5. Share the Staff Generator with staff.

## Staff Generator Layout

The staff form is organized into four sections:

1. Personal Information
2. Building & Contact
3. Website & Scheduling
4. Message, Logo & Options
